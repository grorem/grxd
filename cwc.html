Aligning business goals to our intuition for an inherently better user experience is easier said than done, yet facilitating this communication is one of the primary roles of a designer.

We knew what things about our product we felt are obvious to-dos, but we aren't the only ones who should be involved. While simultaneously conducting studies with students and clients, I led a series of initial workshops with our business and operations teams to go through the current product and highlight known issues.

My goal was to drive the conversation to be about the product as a whole end-to-end experience, rather than a collection of pages and screens.

App recordings.

Having identified affinity of issues across the views for the overall product, as similar issues and thoughts spanned the divisions of discrete UIs, I led a follow-up workshop to explore possible ideas to pursue and come to a consensus of the priority.

This priority, and information we collected from data and recent user studies, then led to a round of initial conceptual wireframes for further exploring possible directions.
