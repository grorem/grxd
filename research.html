Qualitative, Quantitative & Stats

Fidelity for the conversation.

Like prototypes, and often I use prototypes to test, there is a range of fidelity in research I feel appropriate depending on the goal and the message I want to convey. Not every study has to be a stats-heavy qualitative crunch, nor a jam-packed 45min of task-based qualitative observation.

There are times those are powerful tools, but I enjoy employing lighter weight studies and "sanity checks." With research, many small studies measuring a variety of things can be more telling than a grand mega-study with all numbers and stats I too would be proud of when convincing stakeholders.
