Universitat Pompeu Fabra.

Post-stroke aphasia therapy and game scoring

While pursuing my masters degree in Cognitive Systems & Interactive Media at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain, I was a fly on the wall during my thesis work among a group of researchers engaged in post-stroke aphasia research.

My task was to internalize the concepts, reading galore, and come up with a solution for use in-game by which verbalizations captured by a microphone could be scored and displayed to participants. The solution I created utilized two Python scripts, one to generate a spectrogram from captured audio, and the other to then compare these and produce a score of similarity.

I've since taken principles of neuroscience learned and observed here with me into the education industry with EF. Concepts such as, cross-functional links, contextual validity, etc.
